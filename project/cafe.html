About Us

Discover the Elegance of Rzaa Cafee

Founded in 2015, Rzaa Cafee has become a landmark in Solo Raya, offering a unique blend of traditional Javanese hospitality with modern café culture.

Our café is designed to evoke the timeless elegance of old-world charm, with carefully curated interiors that combine classic and contemporary elements.

We source only the finest local ingredients to create dishes that honor Javanese culinary traditions while offering innovative flavors for the modern palate.
